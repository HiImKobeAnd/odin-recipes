Chickpea fritters

  • 1 large Zittauer onion
  • ½ tsp fine salt
  • 200 g Greek-inspired yogurt (10%)
  • 1 ½ dl chickpea flour or other pea flour
  • 2 tbsp grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tbsp red curry paste
  • 1 ½ tsp ground coriander
  • ½ tsp ground turmeric
  • ½ tsp coarse salt
  • 1 can of chickpeas in brine (about 400 g)
  • 100 g finely chopped cabbage or carrot
  • 1 dl finely chopped herbs, such as parsley or coriander
  • 3 tbsp neutral oil for frying
  1. Put flour, vanilla sugar, ground cardamom, salt, and lemon zest in a bowl and add half of the milk.
  2. Whisk the batter until smooth and free of lumps, then add the remaining milk.
  3. Melt the butter in a frying pan and add it to the batter.
  4. Add the eggs and whisk the batter well.
  5. Cook the pancakes until golden.
  6. This recipe makes 8-12 pancakes, depending on their size and thickness.
